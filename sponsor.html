Why do you need sponsorships?

Ignited Minds is a Social Enterprise with the aim and ambition to become completely self-sufficient by June 2017. Our key team are not paid at all and the cost of servers, interns, marketing, development and so on all comes from Campfire. In order to build a platform and organisation that will truly have a long-standing impact in communities across the UK and the rest of the world we need your financial backing to expand our team, develop our servers, extend our service and run local and nation-wide events.

Here's where your sponsorship will go:

  • First £2200 will fund an extra ruby on rails developer to help expand our platform further
  • An estimated £3300 will be spent on marketing materials such as print, social and films
  • An estimated £2300 per month will help us create our own content such as films and written articles
  • £6900 will pay for our yearly server costs
  • £6900 will pay for our first iOS app
  • £12,700 will pay for all of our interns
  • £69,000 will pay for all of our part-time staff
