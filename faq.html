Can I sponsor Ignited Minds?

Business

Yes, yes you can

Sponsorships are what keeps Ignited Minds alive and breathing. Our two Co-founders don't get paid for the 40+ hours per week that they devote to Ignited Minds and it's likely that they never will, instead we want to use all of our money generated through Ignited Minds to better our community. Your sponsorships will allow us to carry on organising events across the UK, keep Ignited Minds live and provide support to startups across Scotland, isn't that great?

You can sponsor Ignited Minds in a number of ways:

  • Sponsor a series or individual films
  • Make a cash donation
  • Your time
  • Desk Space
  • Equipment
